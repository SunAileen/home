Information on Charizard

Charizard™ is a large bipedal orange dragon. It is a fire-type Pokemon with two Mega-Evolutions. Charizard has a large tail with a flame burning on it constantly, and two large cyan wings. It is the fully evolved form of the Generation 1 starter Pokemon Charmander.
Pokedex number: #006 Pokedex classification: Flame Pokemon Type: Fire/Flying Ability: Blaze Hidden ability: Solar Power Gender ratio: 87.5% male, 12.5% female Height: 1.7 m Weight: 90.5 m
